Get help after a disaster

Answer a few questions to see if you qualify to borrow money.

How long it'll take: 10 minutes or less

We're called the U.S. Small Business Administration but after a disaster happens, we support many people who don't own small businesses. If you just experienced a sudden event like a flood, tornado, hurricane, or global pandemic, we provide loans to:

  • people who own or rent a home
  • businesses of any size
  • private non-profit organizations

You have to qualify to borrow money. Answer a few questions about your situation and we'll tell you whether to consider applying for a loan.
